Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Quiz for a Million" (the "Application"), published by AlphaSmartApps, collects, uses, and protects user information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Rating & Audience: The Application is rated PEGI 3. However, our primary target audience is defined as users 13 years and older. We implement a Neutral Age Screen to ensure the highest privacy standards for all users.

1. Data Collection and Usage

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous advertising IDs (for users 13+ only), device type, and OS version.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, levels reached, and progression, linked to an anonymous installation ID.
  • Leaderboard: Your chosen nickname and score are stored on Google Firebase. No real names or emails are required.

2. Protection of Minors (Age Gate)

To ensure compliance with Google Play policies, we use a Neutral Age Screen at first launch:

  • Users Under 13: All personalized advertising and tracking are disabled. Advertisements are strictly contextual.
  • Users 13 and Older: Personalized advertisements may be enabled with explicit user consent.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information. We use industry-standard encryption and secure third-party platforms (Google Firebase) to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

4. Data Retention

We retain gameplay and leaderboard data only as long as necessary to provide our services. Analytical data is typically stored for 26 months. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services & Firebase
  • Unity Ads
  • Google UMP: Consent Management Platform for EEA and UK users.

6. Policy Updates

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
