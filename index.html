Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Quiz for a Million" (also known as "Quiz du Million" in French), published by AlphaSmartApps, collects, uses, and protects user information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Rating & Audience: The Application is rated PEGI 3. However, our primary target audience is defined as users 13 years and older. We implement a Neutral Age Screen to ensure the highest privacy standards for all users.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect limited information to provide and improve the gaming experience:

  • Device Identifiers: We collect anonymous advertising IDs (for users 13+ only), device type, and OS version to display ads and analyze performance via Unity Ads.
  • Diagnostics: We collect crash logs and performance data via Google Firebase to ensure app stability.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores and progression are stored on Firebase. For the leaderboard, only your chosen nickname and score are stored; no email or real name is required.

2. Protection of Minors (Age Gate)

To ensure compliance with Google Play policies, we use a Neutral Age Screen at first launch:

  • Users Under 13: All personalized advertising and tracking are disabled. Advertisements are strictly contextual.
  • Users 13 and Older: Personalized advertisements may be enabled only with explicit user consent via the Google UMP consent form.

3. Data Security and Encryption

We prioritize the security of your information. All data collected is encrypted in transit using secure protocols (HTTPS). We use industry-standard third-party platforms (Google Firebase) to protect data against unauthorized access.

4. Data Deletion Request (Right to be Forgotten)

In compliance with modern privacy laws, we provide users with a simple way to request the deletion of their data:

  • Users can request the deletion of their leaderboard scores or anonymous device identifiers by contacting us directly at alphasmartapps@gmail.com.
  • We will process and confirm the deletion of your data from our Firebase database within 48 to 72 hours.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application uses the following third-party SDKs, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase
  • Unity Ads

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data deletion rights, please contact us at:
Email: alphasmartapps@gmail.com
