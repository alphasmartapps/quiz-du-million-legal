Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 08, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Quiz du Million" (the "Application"), published by AlphaSmartApps, collects, uses, and protects user information in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Rating & Audience: The Application is rated PEGI 3 (suitable for all ages). However, our primary target audience is defined as users 13 years and older. We implement specific measures to protect younger users.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We aim to collect only the minimum amount of data required to provide a functional and enjoyable gaming experience:

  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous advertising IDs (for users 13+ only), device type, and OS version.
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, levels reached, and progression, linked to an anonymous installation ID.
  • Leaderboard: When submitting a score, your chosen nickname and score are stored securely on Google Firebase for public display. No real names or emails are required.

2. Protection of Minors (Neutral Age Screen)

To ensure compliance with Google Play policies and COPPA, we utilize a Neutral Age Screen at the first launch of the Application:

  • Users Under 13: The Application disables all personalized advertising and tracking. Advertisements served via Unity Ads are strictly contextual (non-targeted).
  • Users 13 and Older: Personalized advertisements may be enabled with explicit user consent, supporting the continued free development of the Application.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates trusted third-party services that operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase (Analytics and Leaderboards)
  • Unity Ads (Ad monetization)
  • Google UMP: A Consent Management Platform used for users in the EEA and UK.

4. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • You can reset your advertising ID in your phone's settings (Google > Ads).
  • All local data is deleted upon uninstalling the Application.
  • You may request the removal of your score from the global leaderboard by contacting us via email.
